# EduRearch_AI
EduReach AI: Offline Mathematics Tutor For Rural and Underserved Schools

Statement of the Problem:
Many secondary school students in rural and underserved communities lack access to quality learning materials and internet connectivity. This limits their ability to practice Mathematics effectively outside the classroom.

Solution:
EduReach AI is an offline, AI-powered learning platform that provides structured Mathematics notes, interactive quizzes, and a smart tutor that helps students solve problems across multiple topics without requiring internet access.

Impact:
This solution will improve access to quality education, enhances students’ understanding through interactive learning, and supports teachers in resource-limited environments. It could be deployed in rural schools without internet connection. Behold, this project will help to bridge the gap between urban and rural schools.
